General Terms

15
Aow—The game Art of War
Dev—Game developer
Confa—Confederation
Sopra—Resistance
Rush—Fast attack at the very start of the game
Spam—Continuous mass production of troops
Meat—Using huge numbers of Tier 1 units as cannon fodder
Farm—Base development through strong defense, transitioning to Tier 3 Air Force or Super Weapons
Def-attack / Building Rush—Building defensive structures right at the enemy's base early in the fight
Acct—Game account
Nickname—Profile name
Winrate—Win percentage

Player Classification

7
P2W—Player with heavily upgraded unit stats or who spends real money to fast-track upgrades
F2P—Player with low unit research levels
Skill—Experienced, skilled player with great game sense
Spammer—Player who spams the same unit types over and over
Solo—Player who carries a 1vX match alone
Medalist—Player who regularly places in tournaments
Youtuber—Player who records matches and posts them online

Battle Mechanics

11
Boost—Card granting temporary battle advantages
(to) Gold—Actively using Gold boosts during battle
Counter—Creating favorable matchups against specific enemy units
Full CP—Recruiting the maximum troop capacity allowed by your current HQ level
Smoke—Chameleon's smoke screen
Flask—Wasp's active ability "Acid Burst"
Tap / Click—Screen tap
Kite—Maneuvering to trick opponents or dodge critical hits
Reset—To delete / remove
One-shot—To kill a unit in a single shot
Imba—Overpowered (OP) unit that dominates the battlefield
